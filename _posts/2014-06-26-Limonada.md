### Ingredients

* 6 cups cold water
* juice of 4 lemons
* ¼ to ½ cup sugar, to taste
* grated zest of 1 lemon
* 1 tbsp orange blossom water

### Directions

1. Mix the ingredients until the sugar is dissolved.
